# Chicken Broccoli Bake

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lb chicken breast
- 2 cans cream of chicken
- 1 packet garlic herb seasoning
- broccoli

## Instructions

- Combine chicken, cream of chicken, and seasoning in slow cooker
- Cook on low 2 hours
- Add broccoli
- Cook on low 4 more hours
